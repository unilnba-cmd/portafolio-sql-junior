# Sales Performance Business Case

## Business Problem

A retail company wants to understand which products generate the highest revenue and how sales evolve over time.

The objective is to support management decisions related to product strategy and revenue growth.

---

## Dataset

The analysis uses three datasets:

- customers
- products
- sales transactions

These datasets simulate a small commercial database.

---

## Business Questions

1. Which product generates the highest revenue?
2. Which product category performs best?
3. Who are the top customers by revenue?
4. How does revenue evolve month over month?

---

## Key Insights

• Laptops generate the highest revenue contribution.

• Electronics category drives most of the total revenue.

• Revenue increased steadily during the observed period.
